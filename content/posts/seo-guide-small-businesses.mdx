Search engine optimization (SEO) is essential for small businesses in India looking to attract local customers.

## Why SEO Matters for Small Businesses

Most customers start their buying journey with a Google search. If your business doesn't appear in the top results, you're missing out.

## On-Page SEO Basics

- **Title Tags**: Include your primary keyword in the title tag
- **Meta Descriptions**: Write compelling descriptions that encourage clicks
- **Header Tags**: Use H1, H2, and H3 tags to structure your content

## Local SEO for Indian Businesses

- **Google Business Profile**: Claim and optimize your profile
- **Local Keywords**: Target location-specific keywords
- **Local Citations**: Ensure NAP consistency across the web

## Technical SEO

- **Mobile Optimization**: Google uses mobile-first indexing
- **Site Speed**: Page speed is a ranking factor
- **SSL Certificate**: Secure your site with HTTPS
